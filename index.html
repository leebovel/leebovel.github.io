Ample Hills Creamery

Locations: Brooklyn (Prospect Heights, Gowanus, and Downtown Brooklyn), Manhattan (Hell’s Kitchen and Meatpacking District)

This Brooklyn-born ice cream shop is where you go when you want to feel like a kid again. Flavors are packed with sweet and salty snacks like Ritz crackers, potato chips, cookies, and butter cake … and then can be tucked inside a pretzel cone. It’s one of the most whimsical ice cream shops in the city — and also one of the best. You can also purchase pints at the shops or other retailers around the city, or order online to be shipped nationwide.

Sample flavors: The Munchies (pretzel-infused ice cream with Ritz crackers, potato chips, pretzels and mini M&Ms), Ooey Gooey Butter Cake (vanilla ice cream with St. Louis-style Ooey Gooey Butter Cake), Snap Mallow Pop (marshmallow ice cream with Rice Krispie clusters)
